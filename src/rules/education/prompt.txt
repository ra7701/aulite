DOMAIN-SPECIFIC ANALYSIS: Education & Vocational Training (AI Act Annex III Point 3)

Additionally analyze for these education-specific risks:

1. ACCESS TO EDUCATION (Annex III 3(a)):
   - AI determining access or admission to educational/vocational training institutions
   - Automated admission, rejection, or exclusion decisions require human oversight
   - EU Charter Art. 14 guarantees the right to education

2. LEARNING ASSESSMENT (Annex III 3(b)):
   - AI evaluating learning outcomes, including steering the learning process
   - Automated grading/assessment must not be final without human review
   - Students must have right to contest AI-assessed outcomes

3. EDUCATIONAL LEVEL ASSESSMENT (Annex III 3(c)):
   - AI assessing appropriate level of education for an individual
   - Ability streaming/tracking by AI risks perpetuating inequality
   - Special educational needs classification requires professional involvement

4. EXAM PROCTORING (Annex III 3(d)):
   - AI monitoring/detecting prohibited behaviour during tests
   - Biometric proctoring raises proportionality concerns
   - Emotion recognition in education is prohibited (Art. 5(1)(f))

5. CHILDREN'S DATA PROTECTION (GDPR Art. 8; UNCRC):
   - Enhanced protection for children's personal data
   - Parental consent required for data processing of minors
   - Data minimisation is particularly strict for children

6. NON-DISCRIMINATION IN EDUCATION (Dir. 2000/43/EC, 2000/78/EC):
   - AI must not discriminate based on protected characteristics in educational access
   - Student profiling by race, ethnicity, disability, or socioeconomic status is prohibited
   - Predictive models must not exclude students from opportunities
